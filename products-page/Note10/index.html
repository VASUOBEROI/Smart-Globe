Galaxy Note 10

Samsung Galaxy Note 10 is powered by a 1.9GHz octa-core Samsung Exynos 9825 processor that features 4 cores clocked at 1.9GHz, 2 cores clocked at 2.4GHz and 2 cores clocked at 2.7GHz. It comes with 8GB of RAM. The Samsung Galaxy Note 10 runs Android 9 Pie and is powered by a 3500mAh battery.
